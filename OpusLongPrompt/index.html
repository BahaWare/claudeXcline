Frequently Asked Questions

What is CloudSync Pro?

CloudSync Pro is a comprehensive SaaS platform that helps businesses streamline their operations through real-time data synchronization, advanced analytics, and seamless integrations with popular business tools.

How secure is my data?

We use bank-level 256-bit encryption for all data transmission and storage. Our infrastructure is SOC 2 Type II certified and compliant with GDPR, HIPAA, and other major security standards.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences in your billing.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all Pro features. No credit card required to start your trial.

What integrations are available?

CloudSync Pro integrates with over 1000+ popular business tools including Salesforce, Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Zapier, and many more. We also provide a robust API for custom integrations.
